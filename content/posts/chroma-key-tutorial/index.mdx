T﻿his video provides:

1. **Understanding of Chroma Keying**: You will learn that chroma keying, also known as green screen effects, is a technique that allows you to replace the background of your videos with just about anything.
2. **Introduction to PowerDirector**: You will be introduced to PowerDirector, a professional-grade video editing app available for both Android and iOS. This powerful tool is not just for cutting and trimming videos, but it also has an easy-to-use green screen replacement feature.
3. **Practical Application of Chroma Keying**: You will learn how to use chroma keying. This includes downloading the app, setting up your account, importing footage, selecting the color to remove using the Chroma Key effect, and exporting your footage.
4. **Implications of Chroma Keying**: You will understand the significant implications of chroma keying for the field of humanities. It can bring art, history, and literature to life by allowing students to ‘visit’ historical places or ‘meet’ characters from literature right from their living rooms.
